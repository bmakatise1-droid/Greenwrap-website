Our Story

GreenWrap was founded in 2022 by two environmental scientists who saw how small businesses struggled to afford sustainable packaging. They created GreenWrap to make eco-friendly packaging accessible, stylish, and truly compostable.

Today, we partner with over 500 small businesses across the country, helping them reduce plastic waste and build a greener future.
