Our Business Model

GreenWrap generates revenue through multiple channels, ensuring sustainable growth while keeping products affordable.

Revenue Streams

  • Direct Sales: One-time purchases from our online store.
  • Subscription Plans: Monthly delivery of essential packaging at a 10% discount.
  • Bulk Discounts: Special rates for businesses ordering large quantities.
  • Custom Branding: Fees for custom-printed packaging with client logos.

Why It Works

By combining e-commerce with a subscription model, we create recurring revenue and predictable demand. Our commitment to sustainability attracts eco-conscious businesses, and our competitive pricing ensures loyalty.
